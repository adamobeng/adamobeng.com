Hi!

I'm Adam Obeng.

I like understanding things.

Often the best way to understand things is to take them apart and put them back together again.

Unfortunately, human societies are much more complicated than your parents' alarm clock.

So I make models of societies instead.

It's easier in the long run, and you don't have to scrabble round on the floor for hours trying to find the bit that went sproing.

This website is supposed to give some examples of the things I get up to — sociological and otherwise. The projects here presented are of varying substantive importance and at various stages of completion, but they should give a good cross-sectional overview of the types of thing that interest me. Some of this information is presented in a more formal manner on my CV. If you'd like even more information, feel free to contact me

Still, take a look at some of the things I've been up to, and, you know, give me a job...
